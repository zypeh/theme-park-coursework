About Twinkle Park

We are the one-stop place for fun over 20 attractions spread across 10 acres, Twinkle park provides the ultimate theme park experience in 6 adventure zones — Water Park, Amusement Park, Extreme Park, Show Park. Come experience them all by booking your tickets online.

Once you step in, you can spend the entire day here! Explore the various dining options available at the theme park. If you need some place to sleep, we are just next door to the Twinkle Star Resort Hotel & Spa.

We also play host to some of the hottest acts and global events so stay tuned for upcoming events and promotion by following Twinkle Park on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram.
